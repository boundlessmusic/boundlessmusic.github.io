Blogs

Blogs are some of the best resources available for finding new music, especially music which isn't on streaming sites (see: a majority of non-western music). Blogs sometimes specialize in sharing music from a certain region in the world, be it a country or a continent. They are filled with knowledge, and I use them constantly when discovering new music.
